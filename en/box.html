The Jeedom Mini+ Box

The Jeedom Mini+ is an affordable home automation box. The goal of the creation of this Jeedom box is to offer a simple home automation solution for everyone that’s, above all, at a very low price to make home automation accessible to everyone! Here, there are no enhanced hardware features, but access to the Jeedom software is complete, with no restrictions. All of Jeedom’s software features are there. With the Mini+, the user can put together the kit of their choice, Z-Wave+, En Ocean or RFX.com. If you want to make it compatible with these protocols or others, just download the appropriate plugins and purchase the necessary equipment. When it comes to performance, the Jeedom Mini+ is up to 6 times more powerful than a Raspeberry B thanks to its JeedomBoard Uno board. This performance improvement allows Jeedom to operate comfortably, but it’s best to dedicate it to small home automation systems.

  • 1 GHz ARM A9 Mono-Core Processor
  • 512 MB of DDR3 RAM
  • 2 External USBs
  • Power Service Pack

Jeedom Center Box

The Jeedom Center Box is the reference box for the Jeedom software. This box is compatible with main, current protocols (Z-Wave, RFXcom, EnOcean, and optionally Edisio or SMS key) for a complete user experience guaranteeing a careful design. The positioning of the Jeedom Center is modeled off the Jeedom software, flexible and personalizable. Thanks to its sophisticated design, the Jeedom Center Box is suitable for all environments. It becomes a decorative object in your living room, or a discreet box next to your telephone. The Jeedom software is perfectly optimized for your box, preinstalled and preconfigured for more simplicity and enjoyment when it arrives.

The Jeedom Center box will be offered for sale shortly.


  • 1 GHz ARM A9 Dual-Core Processor
  • 1024 MB of DDR3 RAM
  • 2 external USBs and 2 internal USB ports (places for home automation USB keys)
  • 3 external terminal blocks with dry contact ON/OFF relays
  • Multi-color LEDs for indications
  • 30 GB SSD hard drive 500 MB/s
  • RTC Clock
  • Wifi 802.1 b/g/n (auto switch cable/wifi) + Bluetooth V 4.0
  • UPS battery backup power
  • Power Service Pack



The Jeedom Pro+ Box

The Jeedom Pro+ Bos is the top of the range Jeedom box, drawing on the Jeedom Center’s concepts: compatible with most protocols (Z-Wave, RFXcom, EnOcean, Edisio, etc.), adjustable an personalizable. Lam Pro+ is the remote mode’s native usage. This mode lets you separate Jeedom’s different function on several devices (processor board) called blocks. These functions are general the management of home automation protocols. Each protocol can be managed independently from the other protocols and the core (the central box) with Jeedom Pro+ by a “satellite” block. The use of this remote mode has many advantages, like enhanced performance, responsiveness, and reliability. It’s possible to place the satellite block in a different location than the box for better coverage. For non-networking protocols (RFXcom, EnOcean…), it’s possible to have several satellites to increase coverage.
